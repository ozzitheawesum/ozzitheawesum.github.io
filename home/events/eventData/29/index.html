Futsal Fiesta

PKR 3000

Description keyboard_arrow_down

The mesmerizing art of humiliating and out scoring your opponent in the confined space allotted is indeed an enchanting one. The Olympiad is providing you a chance to prove your mettle among the very best in the twin cities so what better time to unleash your inner Neymar Jr? Brace yourselves for some tough competition because no one will be MESSIng around.

Rules keyboard_arrow_down

Each team can have a maximum of 8 players
  • Each team starts with 4 outfield player and a goalie
  • No limit to the number of substitutions a team can make
  • Substitutions will be made without stopping the game (rolling substitution)
  • Two halves lasting 15 minutes each, with a 5-minute break for half-time
  • The clock stops whenever the ball goes out of play or there is a break in play
  • The clock only starts again when play resumes
  • Fouls are penalised by a free kick direct/indirect or a penalty kick as the referee finds suitable
  • Within 15 seconds a free kick/kick in/goal kick taker must pass the ball, punishment will be decided by the refree
  • Fouls can be sanctioned with a yellow or red card, as the referee finds suitable
  • If a player is shown red card his team will have to play without him for 4 minutes. His team will be playing with 4 on field players excluding him. After the 4 minute duration the team's coach can substitute any player he wishes for the remaining time
  • Argument with the referee will lead to yellow card and Red card successively
  • Disqualification Scenarios
  • Not showing up on time for your match (other team gets a walkover)
  • A Player can only play from one team. If any team found guilty of playing a player of other participant team, team will be disqualified.
  • Player or team with violent code of conduct or abusive behaviour against any player, team or Referee will be disqualified from the tournment
